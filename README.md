# 🏏 Cricza – Cricket Turf Booking Platform

> **A full-stack cricket turf booking platform built with Python, Flask, REST APIs, Jinja2, and PostgreSQL.**

## 📌 The Problem

Finding a cricket turf is often frustrating.

* Players have to call multiple turf owners to check availability.
* Turf owners struggle to manage bookings, offers, and payments from different platforms.
* There is no single place to discover, book, and manage cricket turfs.

## 💡 The Solution

**Cricza** connects players and turf owners on one platform.

Players can search nearby turfs, check availability, and book online, while turf owners can manage their business through a simple dashboard.

## ✨ Key Features

### 👤 For Players

* Search turfs by **name** or **PIN code**
* View available slots
* Book turfs online
* Secure payments with Razorpay
* View booking history

### 🏟️ For Turf Owners

* List and manage turfs
* Update pricing and availability
* Create seasonal offers
* Manage bookings
* Receive online payments
